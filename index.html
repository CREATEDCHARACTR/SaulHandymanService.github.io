Home Maintenance Tips

Expert recommendations to keep your home in great condition. For professional help with any of these tasks, text us at +1 (877) 780-4236.

Change HVAC Filters Monthly

Replace or clean air filters every 1-3 months depending on usage and pets. Clean filters improve air quality and system efficiency.

Clean Gutters Twice a Year

Clean gutters in late spring and early fall to prevent water damage. Remove leaves, debris, and check for proper drainage.

Inspect Roof for Damaged Shingles

Check for missing, cracked, or curling shingles after storms. Address issues promptly to prevent water damage.

Test Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Detectors Monthly

Test batteries and replace detectors as needed. Keep fire extinguishers accessible and check expiration dates.

Check for Water Leaks Regularly

Inspect faucets, pipes, and water connections monthly. Fix drips promptly to prevent water damage and save money.

Clean Dryer Vents Annually

Remove lint buildup from dryer vents to prevent fire hazards and improve efficiency. Clean lint trap after each use.

Maintain Your Water Heater

Flush water heater annually to remove sediment buildup. Check pressure relief valve and anode rod every 5 years.

Seal Windows and Doors

Check weatherstripping and caulking around windows and doors. Replace worn seals to improve energy efficiency.

Clean AC Condenser Coils

Clean outdoor AC unit coils twice yearly. Keep 2 feet of clearance around unit and check refrigerant levels.

Annual Fireplace and Chimney Maintenance

Have chimney professionally cleaned yearly. Remove ashes after each use and check damper operation.

Inspect and Clean Bathroom Ventilation

Clean bathroom fans and exhaust vents to prevent mold. Check caulking around tubs and showers monthly.

Test Garage Door Safety Features

Test auto-reverse feature monthly. Lubricate moving parts and check door balance annually.

Trim Trees and Shrubs

Keep branches away from roof and power lines. Trim vegetation to prevent pest problems and foundation issues.

Check Water Pressure

Test water pressure with gauge. High pressure can damage plumbing - install pressure regulator if needed.

Deep Clean Kitchen Appliances

Clean refrigerator coils every 6 months. Descale dishwashers and clean disposal units regularly for optimal performance.
